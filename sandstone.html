Quartzite & Sandstone

Quartzites are some of the most spectacular natural stones found in the world. From exotic colors and patterns to beautiful, clean white or gray stones, quartzites are in clear demand in the stone business.

While similar to marble in look, quartzites are harder and more stain resistant stones. Their durability make quartzites a great alternative to granites and marbles for making kitchen countertops and vanity tops.

Dimensions for Quartzite & Sandstone
  • Quartzite is avaliable in slabs, half slabs and tiles.
  • Quartzite tiles are available in all standard sizes.
Surface finishing for Quartzite & Sandstone
  • Surface finishing: Honed | Polished | Brushed | Leather
Thickness for Quartzite & Sandstone
  • Tiles: 1,3cm thickness
  • Quartzite slabs: 2cm and 3cm
